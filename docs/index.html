Aark Electrolysis will be reopening on May 5th, in accordance with Governor Walz's directives. I will be using the following guidance, as recommended by the CDC:

  1. Temperatures will be administered five minutes before clients enters my office. Clients experiencing a fever will be asked to reschedule.
  2. Clients will wash their hands before their treatment begins.
  3. Clients will be offered the opportunity to wash their hands after treatment.
  4. Young Living products will be used for my standard of hygiene, with added cleaning products.
  5. All equipment will be sanitized before and after clients, in addition to the usual sterilization. I will be booking extra time to make this happen, and thank you for your patience if this results in any delays.
  6. I will be personally be wearing protective CDC recommended gear, including mask and gloves.
  7. I ask that clients experiencing symptoms of the COVID-19 to please stay home and get medical help if or when necessary. This protects not only yourself, but myself as well as my other clients.
  8. I will not be working around any open orifice. This includes the mouth, eyes and nose. These protocols will be adhered to until further notice.
  9. Anyone that has contracted COVID-19 must be cleared by having a test done which shows negative for the COVID-19 virus. Proof of this must be made available to me before I will work on a client.

As a small business owner, I appreciate each and everyone of my clients. This is a time of watching out for one another and being proactive. We will get through this challenge in our lives by putting one foot in front of the other.
