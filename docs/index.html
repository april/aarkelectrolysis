Welcome to the business of permanent hair removal!

I’m Diane Christofferson, and I have had my electrolysis business in the Twin Cities area since 1990. I was first introduced to permanent hair removal when I was a Cosmetologist in 1978. I attended St. Cloud Electrology School and became certified and began practicing the same year.

Being a Licensed Cosmetologist and Esthetician, my interests lie in self-improvement. I believe in continuing education and professional service to my customers. My mission is to:

  • Professionally remove unwanted hair permanently
  • Use the highest quality of sterilization procedures
  • Continuously educate myself in electrolysis and other forms of permanent hair removal
  • Provide friendly service and respect my clients wishes, regardless of their gender or gender presentation
Five important reasons for patronizing me:
  1. Free consultations
  2. Flexible hours for all clients
  3. Reasonable one-hour pricing, along with package savings
  4. Every client will be satisfied with each service
  5. Free off-street parking that allows privacy for each client
